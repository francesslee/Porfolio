About Me

I’m Frances Lee, a User Experience designer living in beautiful Seattle, WA. I am passionate about creating data-driven, functional solutions to unique design challenges. I do this by gathering insights from diverse audiences, testing researched UI patterns, and remembering to Keep It Simple. When I’m not designing for the web, you can find me drawing ink comics, writing papers on Cultural Studies, or strumming my ukelele beside Lake Washington.
