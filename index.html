About Me

I’m Frances Lee, a User Experience designer and artist. I'm passionate about creating data-driven solutions to unique design challenges for inspired products. I gather insights from diverse audiences, test UI patterns, and strive to Keep it Simple.

I'm a native Texan enjoying life in lush Seattle. When I’m not designing for the web, you can find me writing, drawing comics, wrangling leather or strumming my ukelele on the shores of Lake Washington.
